In Impress, expands animations into static slides, removes animations from the expanded presentation, and exports both editable ODP and PDF files. Includes a headless macro for command-line use (Tools>>Add-ons>>Expand animations).

Issue tracker: https://github.com/monperrus/ExpandAnimations/issues
